# Phase 1: Intelligence Gathering

Complete this phase using web research tools (Firecrawl, Brave Search, web_fetch) to build a comprehensive intelligence report.

## Step 1.1 — Business Deep-Dive

Using the provided URL/business name, thoroughly research and document:

### Core Business Intelligence
- **Core offer** — What do they sell/provide?
- **Positioning** — How do they differentiate themselves?
- **Unique value proposition** — What makes them different from competitors?
- **Brand voice** — Formal, casual, edgy, warm? Analyze their existing copy.

### Target Audience
- **Demographics** — Age range, location, income level, occupation
- **Pain points** — What problems are they trying to solve?
- **Aspirations** — What do they want to achieve?
- **Content preferences** — What formats do they engage with most?

### Brand Identity
- **Visual style** — Colors, fonts, imagery style
- **Logo/wordmark** — Save any brand assets found
- **Existing content themes** — What topics do they consistently post about?

### Instagram Presence Analysis
If they have an Instagram account:
- **Follower count**
- **Following count**
- **Post frequency** (posts per week)
- **Top-performing posts** (highest engagement from recent 20 posts)
  - What formats work best? (single image, carousel, reels)
  - What topics get the most engagement?
  - What time do they typically post?
- **Bio and link** — How do they describe themselves?
- **Story highlights** — What categories do they emphasize?

If they DON'T have Instagram or it's minimal:
- Note this gap
- Research their presence on other platforms (website, LinkedIn, Facebook)
- Analyze their competitors' Instagram presence more heavily

### Key Topics & Authority Areas
- What topics are they known for?
- What topics do they WANT to be known for?
- What expertise can they claim credibly?

## Step 1.2 — Competitor Content Audit

Identify the **top 5 competitors** or similar brands in this niche on Instagram.

Selection criteria:
- Similar target audience
- Comparable business size or slightly larger (aspirational comparison)
- Active Instagram presence (posting at least 3x/week)

### For Each Competitor

#### Analyze Their 10 Most Recent Carousel Posts
- **Engagement rates** — likes + comments + shares relative to follower count
- **Carousel format** — How many slides? Text-heavy or image-heavy?
- **Content angles** — Educational? Behind-the-scenes? How-to? Inspirational?

#### Identify Highest-Engagement Carousels
For the top 3 performing carousels from each competitor:

**Hook Styles (Slide 1):**
- What format? (Question, bold claim, numbered list, before/after, myth-busting)
- What emotional trigger? (Fear, curiosity, aspiration, validation)
- Visual treatment? (Text-only, text overlay on image, full graphic design)

**Visual Patterns:**
- **Color palette** — Specific hex codes if discernible, or general color families
- **Fonts** — Sans-serif vs serif, bold vs light, all-caps vs sentence case
- **Layout style** — Centered, left-aligned, grid-based, asymmetric
- **Image vs illustration vs text-only** — What ratio?
- **Consistency** — How much variation between slides?

**Content Angles:**
- What topics get the most engagement?
- What formats? (Listicles, step-by-step, myths vs facts, case studies, frameworks)
- What value proposition? (Save time, make money, avoid mistakes, understand complex topics)

### Document Patterns

Create a summary table:

```markdown
| Competitor | Top Carousel Topic | Hook Format | Engagement Rate | Visual Style |
|------------|-------------------|-------------|----------------|--------------|
| @example1  | "5 mistakes..."   | Numbered list | 8.2% | Bold sans-serif, yellow/black |
| @example2  | "How to..."       | Promise      | 6.5% | Serif fonts, muted tones |
```

### Identify Gaps

What is NO ONE doing well?
- Topics that get engagement but poor execution
- Formats that are underutilized
- Visual styles that would stand out
- Angles that competitors are missing

## Step 1.3 — Trend Intelligence

Research what is currently trending on Instagram in this niche.

### Trending Hashtags

Identify 30-40 hashtags across three tiers:

**Tier 1: Niche-specific (100K–500K posts)**
- High relevance to the business
- Active community (recent posts in last hour)
- Mix of industry terms and audience pain points

**Tier 2: Mid-range (500K–2M posts)**
- Broader topics related to the niche
- Strong engagement visible on top posts
- Used by successful competitors

**Tier 3: Broad (2M+ posts)**
- General lifestyle or industry hashtags
- High volume but still relevant
- Used strategically for discoverability

For each hashtag:
- **Post count**
- **Top post format** (carousel, reel, single image)
- **Engagement pattern** (what gets to "top posts"?)

### Viral Carousel Formats

Analyze top-performing carousels in this niche from the past 30 days:

**Content formats trending now:**
- "X things nobody tells you about Y"
- "The Z method to [outcome] in [timeframe]"
- "Stop doing X. Do Y instead."
- "Myth vs Reality" — side-by-side comparisons
- "Before vs After" — transformation stories
- Frameworks (matrices, diagrams, flowcharts)

**Visual trends:**
- Minimalist vs maximalist design
- Monochrome vs colorful
- Photography vs graphics vs mixed
- Typography-dominant vs image-dominant

**Structure trends:**
- How many slides? (Currently 7-10 is optimal)
- Slide 1 format? (Question hooks are surging)
- CTA placement? (Slide 8 or 9, rarely last)

### Optimal Posting Times

Research when this audience is most active:

**General Instagram best practices:**
- Weekdays: 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-7 PM (local time)
- Weekends: 9 AM - 1 PM

**Niche-specific patterns:**
- When do competitors post?
- When do their top posts get published?
- What does the audience demographic suggest?
  - B2B: Weekday mornings and lunch hours
  - B2C lifestyle: Evenings and weekends
  - Parents: Early morning or late evening
  - Students: Afternoons and late nights

### Emerging Topics

What is gaining traction right now in this niche?

Use Brave Search or Firecrawl to find:
- Recent news mentions
- Trending Reddit threads
- Viral TikTok topics that haven't hit Instagram yet
- Google Trends data
- Industry publications
