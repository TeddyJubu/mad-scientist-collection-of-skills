# YouTube Channel Style Guide

## Visual Identity

**Channel:** Real estate investing & AI innovation content by Charles Blair

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Red (urgency, attention)
- Secondary: Yellow/Gold (wealth, success)
- Base: Black & White (contrast, readability)

**Typography Rules:**
- **Large & bold** — readable at thumbnail size (320px wide)
- All caps for impact words
- Maximum 5-7 words per thumbnail
- High contrast text (white on dark, black on light)

## Thumbnail Variations

### Variation 1: Bold Warning
- **Feel:** Urgent, attention-grabbing, "don't miss this"
- **Colors:** Red and black dominant
- **Example hooks:** "STOP Doing This!", "Biggest Mistake", "You're Losing Money"

### Variation 2: Gold & Black Power
- **Feel:** Wealth, success, results-oriented
- **Colors:** Gold/yellow and black dominant
- **Example hooks:** "How I Made $X", "The Secret to Wealth", "Proven Strategy"

### Variation 3: Street-Level Real Estate
- **Feel:** Educational, approachable, how-to
- **Colors:** Balanced palette with real estate visuals
- **Example hooks:** "Step-by-Step Guide", "Here's How", "The Blueprint"

## Design Rules

1. **Host prominence:** Charles must appear clearly in every thumbnail, matching the provided headshot exactly
2. **No branding text:** Do NOT include channel name, watermarks, or any name text
3. **High contrast:** Background must support text readability
4. **Emotion visible:** Facial expression should match the hook energy
5. **Landscape ratio:** Always 1280×720 (16:9)

## Content Guidelines

- Focus on value, urgency, or curiosity
- Avoid clickbait that doesn't deliver
- Match the video's actual content
- Use numbers when relevant ("3 Ways", "$10K")
