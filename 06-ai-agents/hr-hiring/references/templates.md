# Job Post Templates & Evaluation Criteria

## Job Post Templates

Based on Mad Marketing Success standard format for hiring technical roles.

### Structure Overview

All job posts should follow this 6-section structure:

1. **Job Title & Overview** - Company intro, position details, compensation, mission
2. **Key Responsibilities** - Organized by functional area with specific deliverables
3. **Required Skills & Experience** - Categorized by skill type (technical, communication, professional)
4. **Nice-to-Have / Preferred Qualifications** - Bonus skills, certifications, industry experience
5. **Logistics & Compensation** - Work environment, growth opportunities, schedule
6. **How to Apply** - Detailed instructions with required materials

---

## 1. Job Title & Overview Template

**Format:**

```
[Role Title] / [Key Skill Area] & [Secondary Skill]

Company: Mad Marketing Success
Position: [Full-Time/Part-Time] [Role Title]
Hours: [X] hours per week
Compensation: $[X]–$[Y]/hour (depending on experience)
Work Type: Remote

[Company Description - 2-3 sentences about Mad Marketing Success mission and approach]

[Role Context - Why we're hiring, what gap this fills, impact on clients]
```

**Example:**

> GoHighLevel Specialist / Automation & CRM Expert
>
> Company: Mad Marketing Success
> Position: Full-Time GoHighLevel Specialist
> Hours: 40 hours per week
> Compensation: $6–$10/hour (depending on experience)
> Work Type: Remote
>
> Mad Marketing Success is a dynamic digital marketing agency dedicated to helping businesses scale through powerful automation and conversion-optimized marketing systems. We specialize in creating high-performing marketing funnels, streamlined customer journeys, and automated workflows that drive real results for our clients.
>
> Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge marketing technology and strategies that not only attract leads but convert them into loyal customers. As we continue to grow, we're seeking a skilled [Role] to join our team and help us deliver exceptional results for our expanding client base.

## 2. Key Responsibilities Template

Break down by functional area with **specific, measurable deliverables**.

**Format:**

```
As our [Role], you will be the [key function] of our client success, responsible for [primary outcome]:

[Functional Area 1]:
• [Specific deliverable with quantity/frequency if applicable]
• [Technical skill application]
• [Quality standard or outcome]

[Functional Area 2]:
• [Specific task]
• [Integration or workflow detail]
• [Performance metric or standard]

[Functional Area 3 (Optional)]:
• [Advanced or bonus responsibility]
```

**Example:**

> As our GoHighLevel Specialist, you will be the technical backbone of our client success, responsible for building and optimizing the systems that drive our clients' growth:
>
> **Funnel & Landing Page Development:**
> • Design and build 15-20 high-converting landing pages and sales funnels per month using GoHighLevel
> • Create custom graphics, banners, and visual elements to enhance page aesthetics and conversions
> • Migrate existing websites and funnels into GoHighLevel while maintaining design integrity and functionality
> • Ensure all pages are mobile-responsive, fast-loading, and optimized for conversions
>
> **Marketing Automation & Workflow Creation:**
> • Build comprehensive automation sequences including nurture campaigns, appointment reminders, and purchase follow-ups
> • Create and manage email and SMS marketing campaigns with proper segmentation and timing
> • Develop drip sequences that guide prospects through the customer journey
>
> **System Integration & Technical Support:**
> • Integrate GoHighLevel with third-party tools including Zapier, Calendly, Stripe, and other essential business applications
> • Troubleshoot technical issues related to automations, integrations, and platform functionality

## 3. Required Skills & Experience Template

Organize by skill category. Always include specific experience requirements and technical depth.

**Format:**

```
**Essential [Domain] Expertise:**
• Minimum [X]+ years of hands-on experience with [platform/tool]
• Proven track record of [specific achievement]
• Deep understanding of [core competency]
• Experience with [key processes]

**Technical [Integration/Development] Skills:**
• Strong experience with [tool] for [purpose]
• Knowledge of [technical concept]
• Ability to [problem-solving skill]

**[Marketing/Design/Domain] Understanding:**
• Solid grasp of [key principles]
• Understanding of [methodology]
• Experience with [practical application]

**Communication & Professional Skills:**
• Excellent written and spoken English communication skills
• Strong attention to detail with ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
• Proactive problem-solving approach with minimal supervision required
• Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical clients clearly
```

**Always Include These Core Requirements:**
- Fluent English (written & spoken)
- Attention to detail
- Ability to work independently
- Own computer
- Strong internet connection

## 6. How to Apply Template

**Format:**

```
We're looking for a dedicated professional who is passionate about [domain] and committed to delivering exceptional results. To be considered for this position, please submit the following:

**Required Application Materials:**

1. **Resume or Experience Summary:**
   • Detailed description of your [key skill] experience and expertise
   • List of previous roles involving [relevant work]
   • Any relevant certifications or training completed

2. **Portfolio:**
   • Provide 3-5 specific examples of [work type] you've completed
   • Include screenshots or links to [deliverables]
   • Describe the objectives, your approach, and results achieved for each project
   • If possible, include before/after metrics showing improvements you implemented

3. **[Technical/Design] Demonstration:**
   • Brief description of the most complex [project type] you've created
   • Explain a challenging [problem] you solved and your approach
   • Share an example of how you [specific achievement]

4. **Case Study (Optional but Preferred):**
   • Detailed breakdown of a successful [domain] project
   • Include the client's goals, your strategy, implementation process, and measurable results

**Application Instructions:**
• Subject line: "[Role] Application - [Your Name]"
• Include your expected start date and availability
• Mention your current time zone and preferred working hours

**What Happens Next:**
1. Initial application review (we'll respond within 3-5 business days)
2. Technical skills assessment via video call
3. Practical test project
4. Final interview with team leads
5. Reference checks and offer discussion

We value quality over quantity in applications. Take the time to showcase your expertise and demonstrate why you're the right fit for Mad Marketing Success.
```

## Candidate Evaluation Criteria

### Evaluation Framework

When evaluating candidates in Phase 2, use this structured framework:

#### 1. **Technical Fit (40%)**
- Do they have the required skills and experience?
- Is their past work relevant to this role?
- Do they demonstrate depth or just breadth?

#### 2. **Portfolio Quality (30%)**
- Did they submit a portfolio?
- Is the work high-quality and well-presented?
- Does it show problem-solving or just execution?

#### 3. **Communication & Professionalism (20%)**
- Is their cover letter tailored or generic?
- Did they follow application instructions?
- Is their writing clear and professional?

#### 4. **Cultural & Strategic Fit (10%)**
- Do they show initiative and problem-solving?
- Are they likely to work well in our async, fast-paced environment?
- Do they seem genuinely interested in the role?

## Google Drive Folder Structure

When creating the hiring folder, use this structure:

```
Hiring /
  └── [Job Title] /
       ├── README.txt (instructions for applicants)
       ├── Resumes /
       ├── Portfolios /
       └── Cover Letters /
```

**README.txt content:**

> Please upload your materials to this folder:
> - Resume (PDF preferred)
> - Portfolio or work samples (PDF, link, or zip file)
> - Cover letter (optional but encouraged)
>
> Name your files: [YourName]_[DocumentType].[extension]
> Example: JohnDoe_Resume.pdf

## Notion Database Schema

When creating the Notion database for the job post, include these fields:

- **Job Title** (Title field)
- **Status** (Select: Draft, Posted, Reviewing, Filled, Archived)
- **Date Posted** (Date)
- **Upwork Link** (URL)
- **Short Form** (Text)
- **Long Form** (Text)
- **Applicant Count** (Number)
- **Top Candidates** (Relation or Multi-select)
- **Notes** (Text)
